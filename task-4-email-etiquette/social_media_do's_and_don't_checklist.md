# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

## ✅ Do’s
1. Use two-factor authentication  
   Always enable extra security like OTP or authentication apps to protect your accounts from hacking.

2. Respect others’ privacy  
   Take permission before posting photos, videos, or personal information of friends or family.

3. Read before you share  
   Don’t just trust headlines—open and check the full content to avoid spreading misinformation.

4. Be kind and respectful online  
   Communicate politely in comments and messages, just like you would in real life.

5. Share your skills and activities  
   Post about your hobbies, achievements, or projects to create a positive and meaningful profile.

---

## ❌ Don’ts
1. Don’t rely only on social media for networking  
   Real connections matter—try to build relationships beyond just likes and follows.

2. Don’t trust everything you see online  
   Be careful of fake news, scams, and suspicious links, and keep your personal details private.

3. Don’t copy others’ content without credit  
   Always give proper credit to the original creator to avoid plagiarism and show respect.

4. Don’t compare your life with others  
   Remember that people only share their best moments, not their real everyday struggles.

5. Don’t post when emotions are high  
   Avoid posting when angry, upset, or overly excited, as it may lead to regret later.
