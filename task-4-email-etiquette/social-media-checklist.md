# **Social Media do's and don'ts Checklist**



#### **Do's**



* ##### Use two-factor authentication

&#x20;  Always enable extra security like OTP or authentication apps to protect your accounts from hacking.



* ##### Respect other's privacy

&#x20;  Take permission before posting photos, videos, or personal information of friends or family.



* ##### Read before you share

&#x20;  Don't just trust headlines open and check the full content to avoid spreading misinformation.



* ##### Be kind and respectful online

&#x20;  Communicate politely in comments and message , just like you would in real life.



* ##### Share your skills and activities

&#x20;  Post about your hobbies, achievements, or projects to create a positive and meaningful profile.





#### **Don't**



* ##### Don't rely only on social media for networking

&#x20;  Real connections matter try to build relationships beyond just likes and follows.



* ##### Don't trust everything you see online

&#x20;  Be careful of fake news, scams, and suspicious links, and keep your personal details private.



* ##### Don't copy others content without credit

&#x20;  always give proper credit to the original creator to avoid plagiarism and show respect.



* ##### Don't compare your life with others

&#x20;  remember that people only share their best moments, not their real everyday struggles.



* ##### Don't post when emotions are high

&#x20;  Avoid posting when angry, upset, or overly excited, as it may lead to regret later.
