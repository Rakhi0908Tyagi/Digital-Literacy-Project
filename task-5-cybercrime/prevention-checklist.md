# \#Stay Safe Online Checklist



* **Check if a website is safe before using it:**

  * Look for HTTPS and lock icon
  * Avoid websites with spelling mistakes
  * Be careful of too many ads or strange design 
  * Use only secure payment methods



* Do not use **public Wi-Fi** for sensitive work like banking; always use a secure connection or VPN.



* Never share your **UPI pin** with anyone; it is only needed to send money, not to receive it.



* Regularly **back up** important data on a secure device or cloud to avoid loss during cyberattacks like ransomware.



* Cover your **laptop camera** when not in use to protect your privacy from possible hacking.



* Avoid **saving passwords** on shared devices and always keep them strong and secure.



* Avoid clicking on **unknown links** or messages, as they may be scams or phishing attempts.



* If you face any **cyber fraud**, report it immediately on [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in) or call 1930.
